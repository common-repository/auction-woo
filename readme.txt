== Short Description ==

Enable your customers to participate in auctions for your products and secure them at the best possible price.

== Description ==

Elevate your WooCommerce store with the powerful "Bidding for WooCommerce" plugin, designed to bring auction-style bidding to your product listings. This feature-rich plugin offers a dynamic and engaging way to sell products, enabling you to maximize profits and provide an interactive shopping experience for your customers.

== Features ==

*Set bids functionality, bid timers, reserve prices to any product or category.
*Notify customers via email when new bids are placed, keeping them updated on the bidding status.
*Allow customers to manually increase their bids or set automatic increments at specified intervals.
*Admins can view highest bids in a graphical format , offering a clear overview of bidding activity.
*Approve or Disapprove bids, view detailed bid histories, and export bid data as CSV files.

== What are the product benefits? *==
The bidding for woocommerce plugins offer a new revenue stream to admin by enabling auctions for select products, potentially increasing sales prices. Admins get benefits from easy bid management, with graphical bid tracking, detailed bid histories, and CSV export options. 

Customers can participate in auctions, ensuring they get desired products while potentially getting better deals. The plugin's automated and manual bidding features, along with email notifications, keep customers informed and engaged. 

Additionally, the "Buy Now" option offers flexibility for those who prefer immediate purchases. Overall, it increases customer interaction and can lead to higher sales.

== Why was this product built? What is the supporting rationale? * ==

Many store owners seek ways to increase customer engagement and sales potential by offering products through competitive bidding. After reviewing user feedback and market demand, we developed this plugin to provide a robust and flexible solution. It offers features like bid management, automatic and manual bid increments, and comprehensive notification systems

but our plugin offers unique features that set it apart:

For Admin:

1: Comprehensive Bid Dashboard: Admins can view bid details in a separate dashboard with graphical representation and other detailed information.

3: Customization Options: Fully customizable bid badges, timer and date settings, timer color, background, and bid/auction end time settings.
4: Bid Tracking on Product Pages: On the backend single product page, admins can view the number of bids when editing the product.
5: Offer short code functionality to place bid option to any place.

For Frontend:

"Buy Now" Option: Allows customers to purchase immediately without bidding.
Bid Management: Customers can see all their bidding details on their account page and download these details in CSV format.
